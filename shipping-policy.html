Shipping Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2024

1. Shipping Timeline

We aim to deliver your products within 7-10 business days across India.

2. Shipping Charges

  • FREE Shipping: Orders above ₹2,000
  • Standard Shipping: ₹150 for orders below ₹2,000

3. Serviceable Areas

We currently ship pan-India to all serviceable pincodes. Enter your pincode at checkout to verify delivery availability.

4. Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are processed same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM are processed next business day
  • No processing on Sundays and public holidays

5. Tracking

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. Track your order in the "My Orders" section of your account.

6. Shipping Partners

We use reputable courier partners including Delhivery, BlueDart, and India Post.

7. Failed Delivery

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or unavailability:

  • Courier will attempt redelivery
  • After 3 failed attempts, product returns to us
  • Reshipping charges apply

8. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping coming soon.

9. Contact

Shipping queries: support@akaraonline.co.in
