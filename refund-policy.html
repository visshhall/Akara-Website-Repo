Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2024

1. Returns

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery as per the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.

Conditions for Returns:

  • Product must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Return must be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase required

2. Eligible for Return

  • Damaged products
  • Defective items
  • Wrong product delivered

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized products
  • Products showing signs of use
  • Damaged packaging due to customer handling

4. Refund Process

  1. Contact us at support@akaraonline.co.in with order number and reason
  2. We will review your request within 24 hours
  3. If approved, arrange for product pickup
  4. Refund processed within 7-14 business days after receiving returned item

5. Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used during purchase.

6. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different sizes/colors subject to availability. Contact support@akaraonline.co.in for exchange requests.

7. Return Shipping

  • Damaged/Defective products: We bear return shipping costs
  • Change of mind: Customer bears return shipping costs
