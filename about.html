Where Geometry
Meets Craft

Atelier Ākāra was born from a simple belief — that the objects we surround ourselves with should be as considered and intentional as the lives we lead. We design artifacts that are precise in form, honest in material, and quietly extraordinary in the spaces they inhabit.

Our Story

Precision Forge Labs

Founded in 2024 under the umbrella of Precision Forge Labs, Atelier Ākāra set out to redefine how contemporary Indian homes think about decorative objects. The name itself — Ākāra, Sanskrit for "form" — speaks to our obsession with shape, proportion, and the grammar of geometry.

Every piece in our collection is conceived through a rigorous design process that begins with mathematical principles and ends with handcrafted finishing. We believe that the most enduring objects are those where nothing is accidental — every edge, every angle, every surface tells a deliberate story.

From our studio in Mumbai, we ship across India, bringing geometric precision to living rooms, dining spaces, and private gardens from Delhi to Bengaluru.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Geometric Precision

Every form begins as a mathematical idea. We translate angles, ratios, and symmetries into objects that feel inevitable — as though they could not have been any other shape.

Material Honesty

We work with materials that are true to themselves — concrete that looks like concrete, metal that bears the marks of its making. No veneer, no artifice, no apology.

Considered Living

We believe the objects in our homes shape how we think and feel within them. Our designs are made to be lived with — improving with time, rewarding close attention.

How We Work

From Concept to Collection

01

Research

We study geometry, architecture, and the proportional systems of the natural world to find the forms worth making.

02

Design

Concepts are developed as precise technical drawings and scaled models, refined until every dimension feels right.

03

Prototype

Physical prototypes are built and tested in real spaces. We iterate until form and function are in complete harmony.

04

Craft

Final pieces are produced with meticulous hand-finishing, inspected individually before they leave our studio.
