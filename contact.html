Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Do you ship across India? +

Yes — we ship pan-India through trusted courier partners. Delivery typically takes 5–8 business days depending on your location. Shipping charges are calculated at checkout.

What materials are your products made from? +

Our pieces are made from high-grade concrete, powder-coated metal, and natural materials. Each product page lists the exact material composition. We believe in full transparency about what goes into our work.

Can I return or exchange a product? +

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items in original, unused condition. Please email us with your order number and photos of the item to initiate a return.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing? +

Yes — we work with interior designers, hospitality businesses, and retail partners. Select "Wholesale / B2B" in the contact form above and tell us about your project.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also log in to your account and view live tracking from the My Orders section.

Are products available for studio viewing? +

Studio visits are available by appointment in Mumbai. Drop us a message through the form above or email us directly, and we'll arrange a convenient time for you.
