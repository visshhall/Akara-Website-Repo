What happens when you delete your account

Permanently Deleted

  • Your profile and personal information
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Wishlist items

  • Reward points and credits
  • Email preferences and subscriptions

Legally Retained

  • Order history and invoices (7 years — Income Tax Act requirement)
  • GST transaction records (7 years — GST Act requirement)

Under Indian law (Income Tax Act 1961 & GST Act 2017), we are required to retain transaction and invoice records for a minimum of 7 years. This data will be anonymised — your name, email, and contact details will be permanently removed. For any queries, contact support@akaraonline.co.in.
