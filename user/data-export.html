Section 5 · Right to Erasure

Delete My Account

Permanently delete your account and all associated personal data.

⚠ Irreversible Action

Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, addresses, wishlist, and reward points. This cannot be undone.

Exception: GST invoices and order records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law (Income Tax Act & CGST Act). These records will be anonymised — your name and contact details will be removed, but transaction data is retained.

Active orders cannot be deleted until they are delivered or cancelled. Please ensure all pending orders are resolved before requesting deletion.
